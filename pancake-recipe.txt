Ingredients:
 - 2 cups flour
 - 3 tablespoons sugar
 - 1 teaspoon salt
 - 2 tablespoons baking powder
 - 2 eggs, beat them separetely before adding to mixture
 - 1/4 cup butter, melted (1/8 of a pound)
 - 1 3/4 cups milk

Directions:
 1. Mix the dry items first
 2. Combine the eggs and melted butter to the milk and slowly stir into flour mixture
 3. Let sit at least 10 minutes while heating the pan
